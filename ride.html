Accurately Predicted Hotspots in the Area

This is a map depicting the most active hotspots in the entire NYC area, updated real-time with Uber data. Hotspots are determined through first a Random Forest Classification model, which are then sorted by relevance and popularity with a binary search and optimization algorithm. Requesting a ride will portray more hotspots in your immediate vicinity.
